ATC control prices in Australia:  letter from the Director General of IATA to the CEO of 
Airservices Australia, Mr Greg Russell  

 
 
 

17 August 2009 

 
 
Dear Mr Russell, 
 
Thank you for your letter of 30 July informing me that Airservices will hold prices at their 
current levels until June 2011. I appreciate you updating me personally on this matter. 
 
I am pleased to note that Airservices Australia is taking appropriate steps in response to 
the harsh economic environment that faces all of us in the aviation industry. I know that 
airlines will appreciate your pricing commitments for the next two years. The economic 
outlook continues to be very uncertain, so it is vital to maintain a sharp focus on additional 
cost reduction opportunities. 
 
We are hopeful that the discussions on pricing models and service charter will result in 
industry leading outputs. Our collective aim should be to develop International Best 
Practice frameworks that ensure cost-effective performance by Airservices and can also 
be used by other air navigation providers. IATA will continue working with you and your 
team to ensure that these objectives are met.  Please do not hesitate to contact any of the 
IATA team if we can be of any assistance in this regard. 
 
 
Sincerely, 
 
 
 
Giovanni Bisignani 
Director General and CEO 
IATA – International Air Transport Association 
Montreal – Geneva
