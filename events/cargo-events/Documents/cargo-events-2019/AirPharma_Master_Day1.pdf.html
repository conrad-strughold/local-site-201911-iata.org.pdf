Single convention on narcotic drugs and psychotropic 

substances (1961/1971) U.N.

Aims of the convention/treaty:

•

To limit the possession, use, trade in, distribution, import, export, 

manufacture and production of drugs exclusively to medical and 

scientific purposes;

•

To combat drug trafficking through international cooperation to 

deter and discourage drug traffickers; 

•

Establish a national agency.
