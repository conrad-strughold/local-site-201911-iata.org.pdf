14:45:   Where are we with test standards for fire-resistant containers, fire 

containment covers / fire containment bags? 

 

Fire containment covers for pallets and fire-resistant containers have the 

potential to offer significant addition protections to the aircraft cargo 

compartment fire suppression system. However, there is current no standard that 

addresses testing for other than a Class A fire (shredded paper and cardboard). 

This session will consider the potential for the development of a standard to 

support growing demand from operators.

 15:30:   e-Commerce and Mail, a Risk or Opportunity?

 

The growth of e-commerce is providing a much-needed lift for postal operators 

globally as many of the e-commerce sellers utilize international air mail to get 

their products to their customers. However, is enough effort being put into 

ensuring that all parties in the process are aware of the restrictions on dangerous 

goods to ensure that dangerous goods do not enter the mail steam?
