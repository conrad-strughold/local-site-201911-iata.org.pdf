To guide air transport supply chain stakeholders in raising the bar in the industry by 
improving the level of competency, operational and technical preparedness in the handling 
and transport of pharmaceuticals, live animals and perishabales. Industry feedback on their 
experiences with CEIV, discussions on the latest project developments and the way forward.
