Why is innovation the essence of humanity 

Jerome Thil,

 

VP Digital Innovation, Singapore Airlines

 

 

Singapore Airlines’ new digital innovation lab, known as KrisLab, encapsulates the 

airlines ambition to be the leading digital airline in the world and empowers all staff 

members to help drive Group-wide innovation. It provides employees with a collaborative 

workspace to develop their ideas further.

 

Jerome Thil has been spearheading the program since it’s inception and will highlight the 

benefits the KrisLab has brought to the company in the few months since opening.  

16:00 – 16:15 

Launch of FACE-UP! 2020: The air cargo career competition  

Glyn Hughes,

 

Global Head of Cargo, IATA 

 

FACE-UP! is a competition for recent graduates to present their thesis at the World Cargo 

Symposium (WCS). The idea is to bring fresh energy into the air cargo industry by offering 

opportunities that will assist the professional development of talented graduates with a 

passion for the industry. Air cargo is growing at an impressive rate and we want young 

people to be a part of unlocking and benefitting from this potential.
