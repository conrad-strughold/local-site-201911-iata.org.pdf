Cutting edge technology solutions, non-technology based solutions such as K9 
and other methods:

continues to be an attractive target for those wishing to cause unlawful interference with 
an aircraft.  Air cargo shipments will continue to increase while still having to comply with 
security screening requirements.  What are some of the technology based, non-
technology based and other means available to support the demand?

Chairperson Closing Remarks

 

 

Future Security Screening Efficiencies & Opportunities 

This track will bring together airlines, International Organizations, regulators and screening providers 
to discuss the future of security screening; what is expected, what is being developed, what is still 
being imagined and what opportunities exist for more efficient, effective and faster screening.
