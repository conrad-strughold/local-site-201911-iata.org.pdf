Objective

The purpose of this study is to measure and compare the 

screening times for 3 different TSA/ECAC approved air cargo 

screening methods in order to identify the most time-efficient 

method among the three.

Defining the start and finish of screening

•

X-Ray: Starts with placing the shipment on a moving 

conveyor belt and ends with new image fully enters the 

monitor

•

ETD: Starts with sampling and finishes with removal of swab 

from the equipment

•

EDD: Starts with the handler’s search command and finishes 

with the sniffing of last pallet of cargo.

Findings and Summary

1. ETD as a primary air cargo screening 

method is impractical.

2. The content of cargo has an enormous 

effect on X-Ray screening time whereas it 

does not much effect on EDD screening

time.

./events/avsec/avsec2019ppts/1530-1700 -Onur Zeyrek - Comparative Time Effectiveness Analysis of Air Cargo Screening Methods - Day 2-html.html

Findings and Summary

3. In all 3 methods, operator competency 

and character effects the screening time. In 

EDD, the character of EDD has also an effect 

on screening time.

./events/avsec/avsec2019ppts/1530-1700 -Onur Zeyrek - Comparative Time Effectiveness Analysis of Air Cargo Screening Methods - Day 2-html.html

Findings and Summary

4. The more the X-Ray 

operator is experienced the 

less time s/he spends 

interpreting complicated 

images such as electronics.   

./events/avsec/avsec2019ppts/1530-1700 -Onur Zeyrek - Comparative Time Effectiveness Analysis of Air Cargo Screening Methods - Day 2-html.html

Findings and Summary

5. Operator/handler 

experience does not effect 

the screening times 

considerably for EDD and 

ETD screening.

./events/avsec/avsec2019ppts/1530-1700 -Onur Zeyrek - Comparative Time Effectiveness Analysis of Air Cargo Screening Methods - Day 2-html.html

Findings and Summary

6. 53 % of mechanical parts 

classified as suspicious.

7. 22 % of electronics 

classified as suspicious. 

./events/avsec/avsec2019ppts/1530-1700 -Onur Zeyrek - Comparative Time Effectiveness Analysis of Air Cargo Screening Methods - Day 2-html.html

Findings and Summary

8. EDD is especially time-

efficient when screening 

mechanical parts and 

electronics.
