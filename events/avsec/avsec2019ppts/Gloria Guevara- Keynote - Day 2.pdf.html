Strategic Priority

Seamless 

Traveller Journey

WTTC is bringing stakeholders together around a 
technology and system agnostic, international 
interoperable approach to a seamless and secure 

end-to-end journey

.

It will be for all travellers, services, and 
geographies, thereby increasing security, efficiency 
and ultimately creating more jobs.

Strategic Priority

2. Crisis Preparedness, 

Management & Recovery

WTTC will ensure that Travel & Tourism is part of 
crisis planning globally, so that the sector and 
those who rely upon it, can be resilient in the 
wake of health pandemics, terrorist attacks or 
natural disasters.

WTTC will take an active role in communicating 
during and following crises which impact tourism, 
and set up specific Task Forces to support 
destinations.
