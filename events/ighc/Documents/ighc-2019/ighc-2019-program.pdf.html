Expediting the Air Cargo Ground Experience 
Although air cargo moves at near supersonic speed in the air, how do you move
the cargo once it is on the ground? What are the issues that are causing ground
delays and what are the ways to improve the processes on the ground for
handlers, warehouse operators and transporters alike? How can simplified and
automated modern payment systems be tied to the handling facilities to reduce
dwell time and improve ULD utilization. This panel will discuss needed changes,
new ideas and current practices that have improved the processes.

Speakers:

Gerton Hulsman, Managing Director, Düsseldorf Airport Cargo GmbH
