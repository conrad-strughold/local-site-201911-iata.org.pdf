NOTICE TO ALL TRAVEL AGENTS 

 

CREDIT CARD FRAUD 

 
 
 
 
 
 

Dear Travel Agents, 
  
 
Kindly be reminded that you are fully responsible for any fraud perpetrated on 
tickets and other documents paid for by a credit card when the cardholder is not 
present in your office to sign a credit card receipt. If you accept a card payment 
over the phone, by email or via the internet and the cardholder subsequently 
reports the transaction as fraudulent you will be liable for any ADM that the 
Validating Carrier raises to you.  
 
Please therefore be extremely cautious when accepting any credit card payment 
without a signature, particularly by an unknown person proposing to use their 
credit card to purchase a ticket for another party.  Moreover you are urged to 
treat with caution any ticket order coming from a party not established in your 
country.  Also be cautious about orders which are placed for groups if the party is 
unknown to you.  All of the above-mentioned scenarios should be subject to 
heightened scrutiny before ticketing in order to minimize your exposure to fraud. 
 
 
Regards 
 
 
Agency Administrator
