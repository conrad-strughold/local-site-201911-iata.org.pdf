Taxes, fees and charges represent a growing and  
increasingly complex segment of overall ticket value  
with changes happening unexpectedly and frequently.  
Therefore it is critical to have the necessary tools to  
stay on top of developments.

By consolidating data from governments, tax  
authorities and over 1,600 airports in 190 countries,  
IATA is uniquely positioned to provide you with the  
most comprehensive suite of tax products in the  
industry. IATA tax products help you reach accurate  
and efficient settlements and substantially contribute  
to your company’s bottom line.

The IATA Suite of Tax  

Products

IATA offers the tools to help you monitor, process  
and settle taxes, fees and charges with speed  
and efficiency.

IATA Suite of Tax Products is the global reference  
for the most up-to-date and complete information  
available for collecting and remitting TFCs levied  
on passenger air tickets.

Through a secure web-based application you can  
easily sort, filter and group data by country, currency,  
and tax code. You can also access real-time updated  
records in a user-friendly environment.

Ticket Tax Box Service (TTBS)

The officially recognised, global reference for  

taxes, charges and fees levied on passenger air  

tickets on both arrival and departure.

Key Benefits

•  Ensures accuracy in collecting and remitting  

taxes, fees and charges for each ticket

•  Tracks and updates changes including  

requirements for collecting, filing, ticketing  

and remitting airport taxes, fees and charges

•  Retains historical records

Revenue Accounting Tax  

Database (RATD)

Customised for interline billing, RATD streamlines  

this specific process related to the settlement of  

taxes, charges and fees, sped up by convenient  

access via the web.

Key Benefits

•  Provides immediate access to accurate industry  

references for fees and charges in interlining

•  Performs searches on standard billing amounts  

by country/airport or by flight segment
