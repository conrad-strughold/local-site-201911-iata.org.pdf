The hassle of invoicing 

Billing and collecting your charges on time 
from operators can be a labor-intensive 
and challenging task. An inefficient billing 
and collection process generates unnec-
essary costs. More crucially, it makes your 
cash flow unpredictable, which in turn 
makes planning development and growth 
more difficult.

IATA E&F started in 1991 to improve 
invoicing and collection of aeronautical 
charges for airports and ANSPs. E&F 
makes this process easier for you, 
ensuring that your relationships with 
airline customers remain as harmonious 
and dispute-free as possible. It does this 
by creating high quality and industry-
standardized invoices on your behalf which 
are easier for your airline customers to 
validate and pay. E&F also helps airlines 
automate their payments with flexible 
solutions. This will provide your organization 
with the 

cash flow stability

 needed to 

embark on investment projects.
