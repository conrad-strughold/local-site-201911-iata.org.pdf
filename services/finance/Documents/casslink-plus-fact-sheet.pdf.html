of Your Business

CASSLink Plus – Route Analyser enables users to subscribe to 
cargo market data from an origin country to any destination. 

CASSLink Plus ensures continued customer satisfaction by 
developing leading edge, value-added services for the air cargo 
transportation industry based on IATA’s expertise.

CASSLink Plus – Route Analyser utilizes CASS data from 
CASSLink (the global CASS processing system) to provide route 
analysis information on four key indicators:

•  Total Shipments

•  Total Tonnage

•  Total Turnover (revenue)

•  Yield

Information search on routes can include any combination of 
airport, city or country for both origin and destination, providing 
thousands of possible combinations. 

Users can also include a "weight break" and to view turnover and 
yield without “other charges”.

Benefits

Gain a rare, increased advantage by knowing your business 
environment inside out. Master how your markets and routes are 
performing from the data intelligence that CASSLink Plus – Route

CASSLink Plus – Route Analyser enables users to:

•  Get fast, accurate data for 60 origin countries

•  Spot trends in cargo sales and volumes

•  Track performance on individual trade lanes
