A unique dashboard with drill-down capability

BSP accounts for one third of all airline ticket sales paid by card 

worldwide. 
The IATA CardStats report helps airlines to keep tabs on this growing 

revenue by providing comprehensive information about their BSP card 

sales. CardStats is:

• A summary of all the BSP card sales, split by card brand
• A breakdown of the BSP card sales by region, BSP and country
• A full list of individual records

CardStats adapts to your needs

Subscription starts on any desired month (e.g. to match the fiscal year 

if not the same as the calendar year). From that point on, 12 regular 

monthly updates will be delivered. 
Specific analysis is run on request, and reports on periods pre-dating 

your subscription can be ordered in a summary or detailed form.
Results are delivered in an easy-to-use Excel spreadsheet format which 

allows you to present the results in any form and shape.

Benefits for every airline

CardStats is the must-have tool as no other system or provider can 

supply such an exhaustive report. 
Better monitoring of BSP card sales means better financial control. 
Having access to all this information will:

• Maximize profits and productivity
• Improve financial control and cash flow
• Enhance the decision-making process
• Identify trends and distribution details
