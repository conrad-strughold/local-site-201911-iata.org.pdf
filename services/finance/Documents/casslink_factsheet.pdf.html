Moving Forward in Billing Settlement

It is time to move into a world of automation and simplicity. Wel-
come to CASSLink.

CASSLink is an advanced, global e-billing solution; an innovative 
web-based data processing and customer management system 
for the interaction and exchange of billing information between 
cargo airlines and their agents.

Innovation is the key to moving our industry forward. IATA’s Cargo 
Accounts Settlement Systems (CASS) was designed to be an ef-
ficient, cost-effective and streamlined settlement service provider 
of choice.

It operates through CASSLink, to replace the problem of the tra-
ditional manual billing process with an online solution tailored to 
business needs; offering the flexibility to receive your CASSLink 
reports from the following three formats: PDF, HOT (.txt format), 
or XLS spreadsheet.

Over 500 airlines and their accredited General Sales and Service 
Agents (GSSA) representatives are already optioning to use 
CASSLink. 

Benefits

•  Airline data input, including file validation 

•  Advance billing reconciliation through daily “transaction view” 

•  Global system access 

•  Customized output options 

•  Credit monitoring / risk warning (CICS)

Special Features include

•  On-line correction module 

•  Customized tax solutions 

•  GSSA specialized features 

•  User level control of system access 

•  Consolidation / multi branch / multi location flexibility 

•  AWB Stock Management System 

•  Courier Billing Voucher processing 

•  Separate CASSLink Import Billing System

By choosing CASS you are already on your way to simplifying 
the billing process. Optimize your time and effort even further by 
choosing the CASSLink system to settle your CASS Billing - free 
for all IATA accredited agents! Non-IATA agents can join at a cost 
determined locally.
