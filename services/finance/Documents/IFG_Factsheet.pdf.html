The Global Payment Enabler

OPTIMIZING PAYMENT PROCESSES  

AND PUTTING AIRLINES IN CONTROL OF PAYMENT EFFICIENCY

In today’s payment landscape, airlines must develop different distribution channels in order to have a global reach. 
For each distribution channel, they are faced with setting up various payment processes. This creates complexity 
and  high  running  costs  while  limiting  the  acceptance  of  new  forms  of  payments.  IATA  Financial  Gateway  (IFG) 
addresses these challenges by streamlining and optimizing payment processes as well as facilitating acceptance 
and control of all forms of payments - regardless of the distribution channel. 

A Neutral Payment Marketplace

IFG acts as a marketplace where all payment service providers are welcome to connect and offer their services directly to 
airlines. Through a single connection to IFG, airlines have access to diverse payment solutions to help ensure a high sales 
success rate. Airlines no longer need to manage multiple complex connections to various service providers but instead now 
have the ability to make one connection to IFG and offer the most convenient method of payment to their buyers.

Key Features

Designed to meet the payment needs of all airline business models, IFG offers: 

•

  A single global connection

•

  Access to all forms of payments through a range of payment service providers

•

  A seamless integration with ticketing systems and various distribution channels

• 

Business Rules Management according to the airline’s payment strategy 

•

  BSP/ARC Sales Reporting

•

  Third party services including fraud prevention, behavior analytics and tokenization services
