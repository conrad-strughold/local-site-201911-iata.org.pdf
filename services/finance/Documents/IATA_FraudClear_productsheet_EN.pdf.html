Airlines and travel agents can no longer afford to use fragmented  

solutions and isolated data sources!

IATA FraudClear

 provides a seamless fraud prevention and management system based on  

3 main components

 developed specifically for the Travel Industry: 

  

1

   GDS and CRS high degree workflow automation

• Reading and management of PNR queues

• 24/7 action on PNRs based on scoring 

• Insertion in the PNRs of automation messages such as: RT (Retrieve), RM (Remarks), RC (Confidential Remarks), OSI 

(Other Service Information), XI (Cancel Itinerary), TKTL (Ticketing Time Limit), SSR (Special Service Request), etc

• Cancel, void suspend PNR and tickets

IATA FraudClear

 connects directly to GDS /CRS being the only system able to also analyze bookings generated by  

indirect channels: OTAs (Online Travel Agents) or other travel agents.

 

 

2 

  Fraud Scoring – hundreds of metrics for direct and indirect channels 

• Device Fingerprinting

• IP (Internet protocol) geolocation / country check

• Velocity check

• Pattern identification

• Negative list cross references, etc

IATA FraudClear

 Fraud Management tool also connects systems such as, but not limited to, Perseuss and Ethoca and 

uses the provided information to enhance scoring.

  

3

   Fraud Management Portal and Content Gateway

• Summary of Office IDs, Pseudo City Codes (PCCs) and display of PNRs.

• Customize rules and strategy per airline, travel agency, OTA or even by Point of Sale

• Constant monitoring of evolving and/or changing fraud trends

• Modifications of rules of the algorithm with immediate effect

• Reconciliation and statistics

• Display of scoring results, etc

As content gateway, 

IATA FraudClear

 enables airlines to export their existing data into the system and additionally take  

advantage of existing data acquired daily through OTAs, travel agents and other airline customers.
