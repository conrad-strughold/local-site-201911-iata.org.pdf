Method of measuring progress on the volume of Non-Sampling Rejections raised: 

An estimate of the percentage of prime billings rejected is computed by taking the sum of Stage 1 
rejections for the past 12 months over the sum of prime billings submitted during the previous 12 
months. The ratio is recalculated at each month in order to monitor the trend.
