Sponsored Members:  An air carrier, or air carrier Subsidiary, sponsored by an IATA Member that is a Member

  of the Clearing House, (a) whose revenue accounting functions are performed by that IATA Member; 

  (b) whose claims (both by and against such carrier or Subsidiary) under the Regulations are the responsibility

  of that IATA Member as further set out at Regulation 4(c); and (c) which has not been suspended or

  terminated in accordance with these Regulations, unless it has met the obligations for which it was

  terminated or suspended.

 

 

 ICH Manual, Part A, Regulation 4 (c)

 

 

 Membership in the Clearing House is open to Sponsored Members subject to the following conditions: 

 

(i)  Interline transactions of the Sponsored Member must be included in the Billings of the sponsoring 

IATA Member of the Clearing House; 

(ii)  Any  Security  Deposit  that  may  be  assessed  upon  the  Sponsored  Member  in  accordance  with 

these  Regulations  must  be  provided  by  the  Sponsoring  Member  prior  to  the  inclusion  of  the 

Sponsored Member transactions; 

(iii)  Billings for all categories of Sponsored Member transactions must be included in the sponsoring 

IATA Member's claim, and the sponsoring IATA Member must accept all categories of transactions 

claimed  by  other  Members,  unless  an  express  written  agreement  to  exclude  transactions  from 

Clearance has been made between the sponsoring IATA Member and the other Clearing House 

Member concerned; 

(iv)  by written notice to the Clearing House Manager, a Member may object to the transactions of a 

Sponsored Member being cleared and, in that case, the sponsoring IATA Member shall cease to 

include the transactions of the Sponsored Member in the claim against the objecting Member on 

receiving notice from the Clearing House; 

(v)  sponsoring IATA Members shall be assessed a special service fee in accordance with Regulation 

40 (b) for each non-IATA entity sponsored; 

(vi)  the  sponsoring  IATA  Member  is  liable  to  the  Clearing  House  for  payment  in  respect  of  the 

Sponsored  Member's  transactions  as  if  they  were  the  transactions  of  the  sponsoring  IATA 

Member; and 

(vii)  the  Clearing  House  Manager  shall  consider  applications  for  sponsorship  received  from  IATA 

Members of the Clearing House, any such application to be approved if it is in conformity with the 

conditions set out at this Regulation 4 (c), 

 

 

and those conditions as established from time to time by the Financial Committee. 

 

 

 

Annual Membership Service Charges and Fees 

 

 Service fees will be assessed against Sponsored Members that are not IATA Members as outlined in ICH 

 Procedure 11(a)(ii).
