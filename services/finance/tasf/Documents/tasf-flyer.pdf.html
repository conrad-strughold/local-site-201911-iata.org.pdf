Around the world, service fees collected by travel agencies from their customers have become the 
primary source of revenue for the majority of travel agents. With TASF, travel agents benefit from an 
electronic billing and reporting system that simplifies the service fee collection process by allowing 
IATA Accredited Travel Agents to charge their fees to their passenger’s credit cards.

Collect your service fees on credit cards, simply and efficiently

The infrastructure you already know

Convenient and fast, TASF enables travel agents 

to collect their fees through the existing BSP 

infrastructure with reporting and reconciliation 

procedures they already know and use. So there’s 

no learning curve. 

A streamlined process

TASF streamlines the process by using GDS terminals 

for authorization and processing of the service fee 

paid by credit card, saving time and money. There’s no 

need for individual point of sales and staff training! 

All major credit cards accepted

TASF makes life easier in every way. With all major 

credit cards accepted by the system, you no longer 

have to enter into individual negotiations with credit 

card vendors and benefit from better credit card 

rates!
