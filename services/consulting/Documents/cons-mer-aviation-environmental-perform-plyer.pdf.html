Aviation Environmental 

Performance Consulting

The aviation industry has dramatically improved its environmental performance, but 
more remains to be done. IATA Consulting assists airlines, governments and industry 
stakeholders in developing and implementing comprehensive environmental policies, 
standards and guidelines to promote environmental sustainability and in identifying 
cost-effective ways to improve environmental performance in the aviation sector.

How can IaTa consulTIng Help?

Our team of multidisciplinary experts has in-depth expertise in addressing environmental performance 
challenges faced by airlines, governments and stakeholders in the air transport industry. IATA Consulting 
assists clients in developing solutions to improve their environmental footprint by drawing from best global 
industry practices. Our experts will assist clients in identifying, prioritizing and focusing resources on 
adopting the right measures to achieve environmental performance targets in a cost-effective manner.

IaTa envIRonmenTal assessmenT (Ienva) pRogRam 

IEnvA is an environmental management and evaluation system designed to independently assess and 
improve the environmental performance of an airline. IEnvA is a voluntary program built on the core principles 
of improving airline environmental performance management. IATA Consulting assists airlines in preparation 
for the IEnvA program by providing in-house expert advice in all areas of airline environmental performance 
management including corporate activities, flight operations, ground handling operations and MRO.
