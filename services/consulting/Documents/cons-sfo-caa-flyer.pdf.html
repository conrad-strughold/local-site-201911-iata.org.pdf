As air transport expands at an increasingly rapid pace, civil aviation regulatory 

institutions face more complex challenges to enhance flight safety and security  

to protect consumers and sustain development.
IATA offers affordable and unequalled expertise to assist you in fulfilling the vision 

of a safer, more competitive and sustainable civil aviation industry.
IATA provides customized training, technical assistance and advisory services  

in a diverse range of management, technical and economic areas to support your 

Civil Aviation Department or Authority in building its capacity and developing its 

policies, strategies, procedures and practices.
