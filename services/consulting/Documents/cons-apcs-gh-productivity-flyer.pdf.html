What are the issues?

Airport ramp operations are very complex. Ground handlers 

must orchestrate a lot of activity within a confined area, around 

a very expensive airplane, in a short period of time. Precision 

delivery and safety rely on how well everyone works together.
In a world of diminishing profit margins, increasing fuel prices 

and intense competition with its associated effect on revenue 

growth, it certainly makes sense to look at every opportunity 

to reduce costs and boost efficiency without compromising 

safety and levels of service.
IATA Consulting’s approach addresses two fundamental 

issues:

 How to turn around aircraft safely in the shortest  

amount of time

 How to use ground handling resources (human and 

equipment) more efficiently and effectively

Ground handling is fundamental to safe and reliable airline operations. Even though 

third-party handling has become the norm in recent years, ground handling still requires 

close attention by airline management. IATA Consulting has the tools and expertise to 

help you ensure your ramp operations are as efficient as possible.
