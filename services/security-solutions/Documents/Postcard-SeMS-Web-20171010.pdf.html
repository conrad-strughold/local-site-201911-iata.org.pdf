IATA

Security Management System

(SeMS) Manual

1st Edition

Security is everyone's responsibility. 

Apply the SeMS Manual to foster all-encompassing security 
processes & culture in your organization.
Developed with our industry partners, SeMS focuses on 
essential aspects of security management, including 
accountabilities, responsibility assignment, risk assessment 
and improved communication. 
The goal of this brand new guidance material is to assist 
entities in building effective aviation security measures 
through a standardized structure. 
The SeMS Manual replaces the Security Manual. For an 
overview of where content has been relocated or kept within 
the new SeMS Manual, please visit our website.

In this new edition:

• 

A brand new form to harmonize security reporting 

supported by AVSEC specialists

•  Airline case studies on SeMS implementation
•  Revised and improved risk assessment methodology 
•  Security objectives and security key performance indicators 

(KPIs) 

•  Renewed guidance on landside security
