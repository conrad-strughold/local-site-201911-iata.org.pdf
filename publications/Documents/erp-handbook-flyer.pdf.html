A comprehensive ERP guide,  

based on industry best practices

Developed by the IATA Emergency Response Planning Task Force, in collaboration with 

IATA member airlines, the Emergency Response Best Practices Handbook provides you 

with a wealth of critical information on the correct response to an incident. 

Designed to give you the tools necessary to create an ERP department from the ground 

up, this handbook is the first of its kind in the world. It is created to be a repository of 

information and will be continually updated and expanded as a knowledge resource on all 

aspects of emergency response.

Your key to creating a robust and  

effective Emergency Response Plan

It takes just one mishandled incident to jeopardize your brand and your customers’ 

perception of your operation.

The IATA Emergency Response Best Practices Handbook is your one-stop shop with 

all the answers. It will equip you with the insight and necessary information to help you 

deal with all aspects of emergency response preparedness.

Features

 

•

Guidance on all the necessary steps to create an ERP department in ten chapters

 

•

Fully electronic version, allowing download and printing of selected sections

 

•

Instant updates right to your computer – if content changes, no need to update or 
download new sections; content is right there – automatically

 

•

Content and structure are being continually updated and expanded as a knowledge 
resource on all aspects of emergency response

 

•

Future editions will be dealing with business continuity, allowing you to effectively 
respond to a wide variety of service disruptions
