Can I connect through an application 
program interface (API) to import air 
cargo rates to my own platform?

New in 2019! You will be able to connect to IATA 

Net Rates via a web service API to retrieve rates 

directly via your platform.

Does IATA Net Rates work for General 
Sales Agents (GSAs) and General Sales 
and Service Agents (GSSAs)?

New in 2019! GSAs and GSSAs, as representatives 

of airlines, can load rates on behalf of the airlines 

directly into the IATA Net Rates tool.

Are worldwide rates available?

Yes. The tool offers airlines the possibility to  

load and publish all rates, regardless of origin  

or destination.

Can shippers see the rates?

For customers procuring cargo directly with 

the airlines, they can get access to their own 

contracted rates.

Can forwarders see airline names?

Yes. All rates are listed by carrier.

How are confidential rates treated?

Contracted rates between two parties are 

flagged as such and are only transmitted to the 

respective forwarder account.

Does IATA Net Rates show additional 
information from the airlines, like size/
weight restrictions, routing, etc.?

Yes, airlines can show all kinds of restrictions 

in IATA Net Rates, but the actual information 

displayed depends on what the airline wants to 

show. In general, forwarders should receive all the 

information and rates that are needed to pick the 

product that is best-suited to their needs.

What are the steps for my airline to 
integrate my rates into IATA Net Rates?

The IATA Net Rates team provides full 

implementation support that covers all the 

necessary steps, like mapping of rate formats and 

web service integration.

How much does the IATA Net Rates 
tool cost?

The annual subscription fee depends on 

company size. Please ask for a quote at 

netrates@iata.org.
