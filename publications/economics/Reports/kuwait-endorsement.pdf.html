Endorsement of the “Agenda for Freedom” Statement of Policy Principles:  
A letter from the State of Kuwait, Directorate General of Civil Aviation to IATA Director 
General, Mr Giovanni Bisignani. 

 

Date: 3 March 2010 
 
Subject: Endorsement of the “Agenda for Freedom” Statement of Policy Principles 
 
Dear Mr Bisignani 
 
I wish to present my sincere compliments and refer to the Statement of Policy Principles 
regarding the Implementation of Bilateral Air Services done at Montebello, Canada on 16 
November 2009. 
 
The State of Kuwait hereby endorses the above mentioned Statement.  Pursuant to 
Article 6 of the Statement, I would be grateful if IATA could register this endorsement 
and publish it alongside the other signatory countries. 
 
Best Regards 
 
Yours Sincerely, 
 
Fawaz Al-Farah 
President of Civil Aviation
