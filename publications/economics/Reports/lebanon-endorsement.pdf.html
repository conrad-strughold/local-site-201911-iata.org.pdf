Endorsement of the “Agenda for Freedom” Statement of Policy Principles:  
A letter from the Republic of Lebanon, Ministry of Public Works and Transport, Directorate 
General of Civil Aviation to IATA Director General, Mr Giovanni Bisignani. 

 

Date: 17 March 2010 
 
Subject: Endorsement of the “Agenda for Freedom” Statement of Policy Principles 
 
Dear Mr Bisignani 
 
I refer to the Statement of Policy Principles regarding the implementation of Bilateral Air 
Services Agreements done at Montebello, Canada on 16 November 2009. 
 
As per the current Air Transport Policy of the Republic of Lebanon, I hereby endorse the 
above-mentioned Statement.  Pursuant to Article 6 of the Statement, I would be grateful 
if IATA could register this endorsement and publish it alongside the other signatory 
countries. 
 
Yours sincerely, 
 
Dr Hamdi Chaouk 
Director General of Civil Aviation
