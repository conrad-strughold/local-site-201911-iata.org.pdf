Endorsement of the “Agenda for Freedom” Statement of Policy Principles:  
A letter from the Kingdom of Bahrain, Civil Aviation Affairs to IATA Director General, Mr 
Giovanni Bisignani. 

 

Date: 16 March 2010 
 
Subject: Endorsement of the “Agenda for Freedom” Statement of Policy Principles 
 
Dear Mr Bisignani 
 
I refer to the Statement of Policy Principles regarding the implementation of Bilateral Air 
Services Agreements done at Montebello, Canada on 16 November 2009. 
 
The Kingdom of Bahrain hereby endorses the above-mentioned Statement.  Pursuant to 
Article 6 of the Statement, I would be grateful if IATA could register this endorsement 
and publish it alongside the other signatory countries. 
 
Yours sincerely, 
 
Ahmed Nemat Ali 
Assistant Undersecretary of Aviation Service
