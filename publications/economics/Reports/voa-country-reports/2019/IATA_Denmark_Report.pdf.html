by buying goods and services

estimated to support a further 

16,000 jobs through the wages 

it pays its employees, some or 

all of which are subsequently 

spent on consumer goods and 

services. Foreign tourists arriving 

by air to Denmark, who spend 

their money in the local economy, 

are estimated to support an

83,000 jobs are supported by air 

transport and tourists arriving by 

air.
