Endorsement of the “Agenda for Freedom” Statement of Policy Principles:  
A letter from the State of Qatar, Civil Aviation Authority to IATA Director General, Mr 
Giovanni Bisignani. 

 

Date: 22 April 2010 
 
Subject: Agenda for Freedom 
 
Dear Mr Bisignani 
 
As the State of Qatar has supported the liberalization of the air transport sector for many 
years, the State of Qatar herewith confirms its support to the Agenda for Freedom, and 
endorses the Statement of Policy Principles regarding the implementation of Bilateral Air 
Services Agreements done at Montebello, Quebec, Canada on 16 November 2009. 
 
We would be grateful if our endorsement could be published alongside the other 
signatory countries, and we appreciate informing the Civil Aviation Authority of Qatar of 
any future events relating to this Statement. 
 
 
Best Regards 
 
Yours sincerely, 
 
Abdulaziz Mohamed Al Noaimi 
Chairman
