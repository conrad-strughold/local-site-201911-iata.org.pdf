Bringing our Members Together

As a representative of 83% of the world’s air traffic, IATA works to streamline 
and simplify the communication and information stream between its business 
owners.

In order to enhance business opportunities for all in the air cargo industry, 
CargoLink was created - to help you locate potential international business 
partner(s) centered on your special requirements.

CargoLink is the global air cargo industry directory and information repository 
for all IATA accredited agents.
