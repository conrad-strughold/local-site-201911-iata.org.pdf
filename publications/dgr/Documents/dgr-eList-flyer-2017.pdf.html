The DGR Data eList is a database derived from the annually produced 

IATA Dangerous Goods Regulations manual - the globally accepted 

field reference source for companies shipping, forwarding, handling, and 

accepting dangerous goods for transport by air. 

Content of the IATA Dangerous Goods Regulations is available in an 

electronic format for license to aviation stakeholders. Critical data from the 

current version of the DGR has been packaged into XLS, CSV or XML 

files for integration into various aviation systems to use the data to support 

the acceptance, handling and uplift of dangerous goods in air cargo.

Choose the best data format for you

The DGR Data eList is available in 3 standard data formats 
emailed to customers. It is produced annually and updated 
when any information changes (e.g. addenda). 

Section 2.8:

 State and Operator Variations;

Contains over 150 records that can be provided in a variety 
of formats.

Section 4.2:

 List of Dangerous Goods;

(Blue pages) contains over 4,000 records that can be provided 
to you in a variety of formats including CSV, XLS, and MDB.

Section 4.4:

 Special Provisions;

The special provisions are referred to in Column M of the List 
of Dangerous Goods and the information contained therein is 
additional to that shown for the relevant entry and contains over 
150 records that can be provided to you in a variety of formats.
