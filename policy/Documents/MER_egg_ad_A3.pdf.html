THINGS SHOULD BE SIMPLE.

Unfortunately today, that isn’t the case. On many international journeys, it is difficult to know what 
rules apply because over 60 countries have their own passenger  protection regulations (and they 
aren’t coordinated with each other). Governments can address this problem by working with each 
other on a more coordinated and organized approach to passenger protection – so passengers 
know what to expect when things go wrong.

For more information, visit www.iata.org/consumer

Understanding your rights as a passenger 

should be as easy as boiling an egg.
