Elephant

Over 5,000 kilos of ivory tusks were seized at airports in 

2016 - this is equivalent to two dead elephants a day 

(Environmental Investigation Agency). 

Tiger

There are only 3,200 wild tigers left in the world 

(United for Wildlife).

Live animals

Over a million illegal live animals were seized 

at airports in 2017.

Rhino

Over 320 kilos of rhino horn were seized at airports in 2017 

– this is at least 105 dead rhinos (Poaching Facts).
