What is Blockchain?

Isn't that just Bitcoin or something?

./whatwedo/workgroups/Documents/Paperless_Conference_2017/Day2/1425-1455_VisionforBlockchain_JetStarAirways-html.html

“

By definition, the Blockchain is:

An incorruptible, distributed digital ledger of 

transactions; one that is “secure by design”.

A ledger that is broadcast openly to all, so that the 

network as a whole can validate.

Now even though the word transactions was used, 

this does not insinuate that the Blockchain is 

financial. Transactions can include any data, an 

individual’s identity, and pretty much anything that 

you would note in a ledger.

It is a cool concept once you understand how it all 

works.

Tim Dickens 

SEP 08 2017
