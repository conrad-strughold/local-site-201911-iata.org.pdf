High level transaction flow for Airline web ticket purchase: 

1.

 

Customers select IATA Pay as a payment method and enters their banking 
information. 

2.

 

Airline transfers payment request to IATA platform (IATA Pay). IATA platform 
performs some transactional validations and identifies the PISP (Payment 
Initiation Service Providers) which will be processing the transaction.   

--------------------------------------- Regulated area ---------------------------------------- 

3.

 

The PISP receives the payment initiation request and performs some 
regulatory checks and sends 

the transfer initiation request to the payor’s bank. 

 

4.

 

Payor's bank receives the request from the PISP and checks in the PISP 
directory if this request has been sent from a provider with a valid license for 
undertaking this action.  

5.

 

Payor’s bank contacts their customer to authenticate/

 approve the payment 

transfer initiation.   

6.

 

Once the approval of the payment initiation is received from the customer, the 

payor’s bank will initiate an instant payment transfer to IATA’s bank accou

nt 

(option 1) or the airlines bank account (option 2).  

7.

 

Payor’s bank confirms the approval of the payment request and the initiation of 

the instant bank transfer to the PISP.   

--------------------------------------- End of Regulated area ------------------------------ 

8.

 

Payment confirmation is sent to the IATA platform (IATA Pay)  

9.

 

Payment confirmation is forwarded to airline. 

 

10.

 

Airline issues the Ticket.
